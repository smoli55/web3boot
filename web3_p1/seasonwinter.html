Whats in season?

‹

Winter

›

Winter in Quebec brings its own set of hearty and comforting produce.These winter produce items are great for creating warming soups, stews, and roasted dishes to keep you cozy during the colder season. What winter recipes are you thinking about trying?
Root vegetables: Carrots, turnips, parsnips, rutabagas, and celeriac.

Winter squashes: Butternut squash, acorn squash, and pumpkin.

Brassicas: Brussels sprouts, cabbage, cauliflower, and kale.

Citrus fruits: Oranges, grapefruits, and clementines.

Apples: Some apple varieties can be stored well into the winter.

Pears: Especially late-season varieties.

Potatoes: Various types of potatoes are available throughout the winter.

Onions and leeks: These storage vegetables are readily available.

Garlic: A staple for many winter dishes.

Cranberries: Often available into the winter months.
